The World Wide Web

A Look into the History of the WEB

Credit: www.definanzas.com

The emergence of the World Wide Web as a powerful network connectivity, enables the world to be in the face of your palm.

Here is the history of the WEB

  • 1960: Packet switching research

  • 1960s: NPL and ARPANET

  • 1972: Satelilite link US - Europe

  • 1981: Access to ARPANET expanded

  • 1981: IP4 published

  • 1980s: First ISPs emerged

  • 1989: World Wide Web proposed

  • 1990: First Browser

  • 1994: Cascading HTML Style Sheets

  • 1995: JavaScript

  • 1999: Internet on mobile phones

  • 2000: 414M Internet users

  • 2008: Google Chrome

  • 2017: 3.9B Internet users. 49% views from mobile
